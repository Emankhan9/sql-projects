# E-Commerce Customer Behavior & Sales Analysis — SQL Project

## Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce customer behavior and sales data using SQL.
The goal is to explore revenue trends, customer demographics, payment preferences, and product performance to extract meaningful business insights.

---

## Dataset
- Total Rows: 17,049
- Period: January 2023 – March 2024
- Cities: Istanbul, Ankara, and others
- Source: [Kaggle – E-Commerce Customer Behavior & Sales Analysis](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/umuttuygurr/e-commerce-customer-behavior-and-sales-analysis-tr)

---

## Tool Used
- SQLite (sqliteonline.com)

---

## Business Questions Answered

**Sales Analysis**
- Q1: Which product category has the highest total revenue?
- Q2: Which month had the most sales?
- Q3: What is the overall sales summary?

**Customer Analysis**
- Q4: Which age group shops the most?
- Q5: Do male or female customers spend more on average?
- Q6: Which city has the highest total revenue?

**Payment & Device**
- Q7: Which payment method is used the most?
- Q8: Do mobile or desktop users place more orders?

**Product & Discounts**
- Q9: Which product category gets the highest discount on average?
- Q10: Which product category has the highest customer rating?

---

## Key Findings

- **Electronics** dominates with the highest total revenue of 10,481,897.65
- **January** recorded the highest sales with 6,905 units sold
- Total of **17,049 orders** were placed with an average order value of **1,277.44**
- The **36-50 age group** is the most active shoppers with 19,596 units purchased
- **Istanbul** is the top revenue-generating city with 5,646,595.78 in total sales
- **Credit Card** is the most preferred payment method (~6,801 transactions)
- **Mobile users** place more orders than desktop users (9,543 vs remaining)
- **Electronics** also receives the highest average discount of 273.95
- **Home & Garden** has the highest average customer rating of 3.93
