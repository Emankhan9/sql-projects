# Walmart Sales Data Analysis — SQL Project

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Walmart sales data from 3 branches across Myanmar using SQL.
The goal is to explore sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance to extract meaningful business insights.

---

## Dataset
- Total Rows: 1,000
- Branches: A, B, C
- Cities: Yangon, Mandalay, Naypyitaw
- Source: [Kaggle – Walmart Sales Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/najir0123/walmart-10k-sales-datasets)

---

## Tool Used
- MySQL

---

## Business Questions Answered

- Q1: Which branch has the highest total sales?
- Q2: Which product line has the most orders?
- Q3: Which payment method is used the most?
- Q4: Do males or females shop more?
- Q5: Which city has the highest average customer rating?
- Q6: Do member customers generate more revenue than normal customers?
- Q7: At what time of day do sales peak?
- Q8: Which product line has the highest average rating?
- Q9: What is the overall sales summary?

---

## Key Findings

- **Branch C** has the highest total sales (110,568.71), while Branch A and B are very close to each other
- **Fashion Accessories** is the best-selling product line with 178 orders
- **Ewallet** is the most popular payment method (345), closely followed by Cash (344)
- **Female customers** shop slightly more than males (501 vs 499) — difference is minimal
- **Naypyitaw** has the highest average customer rating (7.07)
- **Member customers** generate more revenue (164,223) compared to Normal customers (158,743)
- **Afternoon** is the peak sales time with 454 transactions
- **Food and Beverages** has the highest product line rating (7.11)
- Total revenue across all branches: 322,967 with an average transaction of 323
